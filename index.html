SPONGEBOB

Spongebob is a sponge based cartoon character who lives in a pineapple under the sea. He lives among many friends such as mr crabs, Patrick, squidward and many others who all play a part in the show. Spongebob works a day job at the fast food restaurant Krusty krab, and spends his time at home with his pet snail Gary. Him and his many friends get up to all sorts around their hometown bikini bottom, where they cause all sorts of trouble

Spongebob is a multi award winning childrens TV show which has entertained people of all ages for over 20 years

Spongebob has been going for 13 seasons, and has had 3 films made about it. Spongebob is the most viewed animated program in history, gaining over 50 million views per month on Nickelodeon. Spongebob the franchise has grossed over $14.8 billion from its inception making it one of the highest grossing franchises of all time.

Spongebob was created in 1989 by Stephen Hillenburg. Many ideas for the show originated from Stephens education book The Intertidal Zone. The book was created for Stephen to teach his students about marine life, but he ended up taking ideas from the book, and turning them into spongebob. Stephen began developing his ideas into spongebob in 1996, and it was released in 1999.
